Sort talent, not files.
BittyCasting is a simple tool for managing your next creative project without rummaging through your inbox.

We are currently in private beta. Access to the beta is by invitation only.

Features
Organize
We’ll sort for you.

Email, Dropbox, iCloud, and your hard drive—these are fine for keeping track of your personal files, but why should you have to keep track of others’ files there? Let BittyCasting take over, so you don’t have to worry about where you left a file or what it was named.

Share
Never deal with files over email again.

Whether you’re casting a short film, running a talent show, or taking open contest submissions, with a simple link, you can share your project, letting applicants send in a set of required submission criteria, be it an audition reel, a voice over sample, a manuscript, a photo, or just a résumé.

Collaborate
Find the best talent. Instantly.

Even small projects require multiple people, so that’s why BittyCasting includes a robust set of tools for collaboration. You can easily review, provide feedback, evaluate on submissions before making the final call. Plus, you can also invite an applicant to follow up in a snap.

Store
Access from anywhere.

With our cloud storage solution, you can keep your personal cloud drive safe and separated. Go back to an old project and look for a previous applicant who is now perfect for a role. Access the talent’s files anywhere, regardless of which computer you’re on.

About

In 2015, I (Ryan) was working on and acting in a number of indie film projects in Seattle. Sending, receiving and organizing headshots, resumes, cover letters, reels, audition, applications, etc. for multiple characters and multiple projects was tedious and cumbersome. After searching for a proper tool and finding none, I started thinking of a solution.

In 2016, I met with other cofounders -- Kaiting, Greg and Tanner -- at Startup Weekend where we formed a team and won 1st place and the Crowd Favorite Award. Although the idea (flavored water for cats) didn't survive further testing, we clicked and decided to solve the casting problems together.

At this moment, we are working with incredible partners to refine our beta product in order to make casting small projects easy. We sincerely invite you to join us on this exciting journey!
