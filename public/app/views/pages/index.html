Sort talent, not files.
BittyCasting is a free tool for directors, studios, and agencies to recruit talent and manage their files without filling up their inbox.

We are currently in private beta. Access to the beta is by invitation only.

Features
Keep out of your inbox
Store your talent’s files in the cloud, so you can quickly find and filter candidates—anytime, anywhere.
Look professional
Create a custom project page where applicants can view and apply for roles, no email or design required.
Collaborate with your team
Build consensus with your collaborators by favoriting and commenting on the best applicants for a role.
Reach a wider audience
Go where the talent is by simply sharing a casting call on your favorite social network.
