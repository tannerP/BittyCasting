About

In 2015, I (Ryan) was working on and acting in a number of indie film projects in Seattle. Sending, receiving and organizing headshots, resumes, cover letters, reels, audition, applications, etc. for multiple characters and multiple projects was tedious and cumbersome. After searching for a proper tool and finding none, I started thinking of a solution.

In 2016, I met with other cofounders -- Kaiting, Greg and Tanner -- at Startup Weekend where we formed a team and won 1st place and the Crowd Favorite Award. Although the idea didn't survive further testing, we clicked and decided to solve the casting problems together.

At this moment, we are working with incredible partners to refine our beta product in order to make casting small projects easy. We sincerely invite you to join us on this exciting journey!
